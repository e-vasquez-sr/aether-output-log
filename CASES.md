## The Four Mechanisms

| # | Mechanism | Description |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | Research Monopolization | Control the research. Fund it yourself. Own the data. Decide what gets studied and what does not. |
| 2 | Manufactured Burden of Proof | Demand proof of harm. Prevent the proof from being generated. Require decades of certainty before acting. |
| 3 | Credibility Destruction | Destroy the credibility of the messenger. Discredit researchers who find inconvenient results. |
| 4 | Regulatory Architecture | Design the regulatory architecture so the outcome is predetermined. Capture the agencies. Write the rules. |
